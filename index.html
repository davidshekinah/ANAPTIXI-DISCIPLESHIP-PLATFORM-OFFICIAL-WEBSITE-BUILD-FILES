An overview of Anaptixi

Anaptixi is a discipleship and mentorship platform that puts structures in place to ensure the growth of its disciples.

Anaptixi discipleship platform enforces principles to ensure that its disciples are consistent and productive in the recommended growth-related activities such as Praying; Studying the scripture properly; Listening to Sermons; Confessing scriptures and words of affirmation; Worshipping God genuinely; Meditating; Preparing and strictly sticking to daily Schedules that align with your goals and by extension, your visions with the aim to have achieved all at their specific scheduled time.

It is run as a family but yet has a leadership structure in place to ensure stability and sustainability with the lead discipler, Pastor David Shekinah and then the Family Admin – Miss Hephzibah Ajayi, then the Family Directorate – Miss Olaogun Oluwalanumi, then other members.
